মির্জার বাজার ইসলামিয়া দাখিল মাদ্রাসা

মির্জার বাজার ইসলামিয়া দাখিল মাদ্রাসা is an educational institute in Baily Road, Dhaka, Bangladesh. It has 4 campuses and around 25,000 students. মির্জার বাজার ইসলামিয়া দাখিল মাদ্রাসা is one of the renowned educational institutes in Bangladesh. We consider every child as unique and so we maintain inclusive learning-teaching environment at every step in our great set-up. It is a fact now that our results are getting better in the public examinations every time. It has been made possible through our extensive and effective care stretched out to every individual student. Our students conglomerate here from multifarious backgrounds; various strata of the society. They enter the threshold of our strong and fortified home of learning and come out bearing an all-round personality.

Mostofa Mojumdar

Superintendent, Mirza Bazar Islamiya Dhakil Madrasah
